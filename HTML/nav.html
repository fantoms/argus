Navigating

  1. When you first take your web browser over to argus, you will be presented with a log in screen. Enter your username and password, and click submit. see the users file documentation to learn how to configure users and passwords.
  2. You will then be presented with the Top level page.
  3. At this point, it should be fairly obvious what you can click on, and what the various links and buttons will do.
  4. Perhaps you'd care to explore the on-line demo
